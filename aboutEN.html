Mcm's system


In MCM Leading International (MCMI) we believe that not only giving our best natural products but also maintaining green environment and empowering local farmers to bring added value for their economic as well. We have terms called 3P (People, Planet, Profit) for developing market and new products, production technology to be applied and management to keep financial and accountable performance organized.

We understand that people need organic products, that's why it's become our responsible to maintaining the source itself. We arrange farmers to comply Organic Procedures, from the trees to their field. No Synthetic Fertilizer, No Pesticide, and continue with Organic Production Method, and then finally Recognized by International Certification Body. With the help from hundreds of farmers and also thousands of trees in Java Island we produce good quality organic product and bring it to the world.
